Bladderwrack Seaweed Powder

Our premium bladderwrack powder is a 100% natural soil amendment made for cannabis growers. Bladderwrack is rich in beneficial minerals, organic matter, and natural plant hormones like cytokinins and auxins — promoting vigorous root growth, nutrient uptake, and resilience against environmental stress.

Use: Mix 10 grams of powder with 1 liter of water and drench soil or apply as a foliar spray weekly.
